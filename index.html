Our Story

We are thrilled to launch the Robotics Club at Lake Travis Middle School for the 2023-24 school year, led by Mr. Wayne Ostrout, our dedicated engineering teacher who serves as our Robotics coach.

Our club comprises 6 teams, each benefiting from the mentorship of students from the LTHS Robotics Club. Under the guidance of their coach, Mr. Eric Fogle, the LTHS Robotics program has achieved great success, with three teams earning a spot at VEX Worlds! We feel incredibly fortunate to have their expertise and support as we introduce Robotics to the students at LTMS.

STEM Education

The Robotics Club aims to inspire and empower our students by providing them with hands-on experience in robotics and STEM education. Through engaging activities, competitions, and project-based learning, our club fosters critical thinking, problem-solving skills, teamwork, and creativity. By participating in the club, students will develop a passion for robotics and gain valuable skills that will benefit them in their academic and professional futures.
