About Us

The Robotics Club at Lake Travis Middle School is led by Mr. Wayne Ostrout, our dedicated engineering teacher who serves as our Robotics coach. Our club has 6 teams, each benefiting from the mentorship of students from the LTHS Robotics Club. Under the guidance of their coach, Mr. Eric Fogle, the LTHS Robotics program has achieved great success, with three teams earning a spot at VEX Worlds! We feel incredibly fortunate to have their expertise and support as we introduce Robotics to the students at LTMS.
